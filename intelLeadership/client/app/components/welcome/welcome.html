What is intellectual leadership?

An insatiable curiosity and thirst for knowledge underpinned by an ability to seek common understanding in an uncommon way, is the foundation of intellectual leadership. We believe in defining a vision and implementing a business strategy that ensures we consistently deliver progress and remain ahead of our competitors. We achieve this by striving every day, to gain insight and knowledge on all topics and areas of the business and our external environment, so that we can contribute, with confidence and understanding, to the success of Discovery.
